Digital Formal Theme Notebook

Kwaderno is a notebook-style writing platform where students compose essays, reviewers proofread them through uploaded PDF feedback, and polished work can be published for others to read.

How Kwaderno works

A simple writing and revision cycle built around seriousness, feedback, and publication.

1

Write

Students compose essays inside a formal theme notebook UX and save their work as a clean PDF submission.

2

Review

Teachers and editors download the PDF, proofread it offline, and re-upload the corrected copy with notes.

3

Publish

Finalized essays can be published in the public library under writer and proofreader usernames.
