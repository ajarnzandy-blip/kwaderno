Digital Formal Theme Notebook

Kwaderno is a digital formal theme notebook where students compose essays in a focused cursive workspace, teachers provide live feedback using a digital red pen, and polished works are shared to inspire a community of writers.

How Kwaderno works

A simple writing and revision cycle built around seriousness, feedback, and publication.
1
Write

Students compose essays inside a digital formal theme notebook. The experience is designed for focus, featuring a specialized cursive-ready workspace.

2
Review

Teachers use a built-in Digital Red Pen to mark up the essay directly. They can strike through mistakes, insert corrections, and leave personal mentor notes in real-time.

3
Grow

Students open their "Returned Paper" to see exactly where they can improve. It’s a direct dialogue that focuses on progress, not just a final grade.
