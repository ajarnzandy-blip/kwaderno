Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

At Kwaderno, we believe every writer deserves a safe and private space to grow. This policy explains how we protect your data.

1. Children's Privacy

Kwaderno does not knowingly collect data from children aged 13 and below. If we discover that a user in this age group has created an account, the account and all associated data will be deleted immediately. For minor users (under 18), we rely on parents and guardians to oversee their digital safety and privacy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email, and user role.
  • Writing Content: Essays and drafts created in the workspace.
  • Feedback Data: Digital Red Pen marks and reviewer notes.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data is used to facilitate feedback, manage teacher-published content, and display essays in the community gallery when approved by an administrator.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

To maintain the integrity of our educational database, users cannot manually delete essays or accounts. For all deletion requests, please contact: ajarn.zandy@gmail.com

Notice of Irreversibility: Processed deletion requests are permanent. Administrative removal of violative essays is also final. Back up your work regularly.

5. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data. Users are encouraged to maintain personal backups of important work.

6. Contact Us

Questions? Reach us at: ajarn.zandy@gmail.com
